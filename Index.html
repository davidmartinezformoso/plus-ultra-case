🔍 PLUS ULTRA INVESTIGATION 🔍

CORRUPTION • MONEY LAUNDERING • INFLUENCE PEDDLING • GOLD TRAFFICKING

Case File: Audiencia Nacional de España | Judge: José Luis Calama

📋 CASE SUMMARY

Investigation into an alleged criminal organization that orchestrated a €53 million public bailout for Plus Ultra Líneas Aéreas, with kickbacks totaling €1.9 million allegedly funneled to former Prime Minister Zapatero and his daughters through shell companies and influence peddling.

🎯 CASE OVERVIEW

The Plus Ultra case represents one of Spain's most complex corruption investigations, connecting former high-ranking government officials, Venezuelan intelligence, gold trafficking, and airline bailouts. According to UDEF (Economic Crime Unit) reports, the network operated through a sophisticated web of shell companies, offshore accounts, and political influence.

The investigation reveals alleged connections to Venezuelan gold trafficking (5-8 tons), dealings with sanctioned regimes, jewelry found in Zapatero's office safe worth €30,000-50,000, and links to former ETA members and Venezuelan military intelligence.
